This is a site dedicated to my love of vinyl records and overall is a love letter to physical music media and why it's important in our digital age.

My Favorites

CTRL by SZA
This album reminds me of prime high school. It helped me reflect on love, self-worth, and how I deal with vulnerability.

DAMN. by Kendrick Lamar
This album made me reflect on my upbringing. The nuances, the memories, and shared experiences. It was beautiful to hear familiartiy articulated this way.

CHROMAKOPIA by Tyler, the Creator
A newer addition to my collection, this album I immediatly felt a connection to upon first listen. I love musical works that take the audience through a journey; and thats exactly what this album does.
